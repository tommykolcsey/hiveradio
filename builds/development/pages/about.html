About Hive Radio Manchester

Hive Radio Manchester is Manchester Metropolitan University’s award-winning student radio station, broadcasting from All Saint’s Campus in the heart of the student city.

From it’s early days as MMU Radio to going “on-air” in 2012, Hive Radio Manchester has gone from strength to strength to build a successful student radio station.
